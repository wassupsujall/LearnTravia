ABOUT THE EXAMS

GRE

The GRE is a multiple-choice, computer-based, standardized exam that is often required for admission to graduate programs and graduate business programs (MBA) globally.

TOFEL

Test of English as a Foreign Language is a standardized test to measure the English language ability of non-native speakers wishing to enroll in English-speaking universities

IELTS

The International English Language Testing System, is an international standardized test of English language proficiency for non-native English language speakers. I

PTE

The PTE Academic exam is an English language test for candidates looking to apply to universities or countries abroad that use English as their primary source of communication.
